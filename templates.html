Initial Order Intake

02

Update Order Status to Processing

2.1 From the Warhead dashboard, click on the Store icon in the Manage section.

2.2 Hover your cursor over the Store tab in the top navigation menu, then select Orders from the dropdown list.

2.3 Locate the customer's transaction. Hover your cursor over the order row to reveal the action buttons, then click Modify.

2.4 Scroll down to the very bottom of the order details page. Change the Status dropdown from Pending to Processing, and click Save.

2.5 Once you click Save, the customer will automatically receive an email updating them on their order status.

Source Product on Doba

3.2 Open a new browser tab and log in to the Doba supplier portal.

3.3 Paste the Item No. into the Doba search bar and select the correct item.

Place the Supplier Order

4.1 Click Add to Cart, navigate to your cart, and Check Out.

4.2 Important: Enter the Customer's Shipping Address exactly as it appears on the Warhead invoice, not your own.

4.3 Submit your payment to finalize the purchase on Doba.

Update Tracking Information

5.2 Update the Status to Shipped, input the tracking details into the note section, and click Save.

ORDER PROCESSING STEPS

Step 1: Initial Order Intake

1.1 Locate the automated "New Order" notification email from Warhead.

Step 2: Update Order Status to Processing

2.1 From the Warhead dashboard, click on the Store icon.

2.2 Hover your cursor over the Store tab in the top menu, then click Orders.

2.3 Locate the customer's transaction. Hover your cursor over the order row to reveal the action buttons, then click Modify.

2.4 Scroll down to the very bottom of the page. Change the Status dropdown from Pending to Processing, and click Save.

2.5 Once you click Save, the customer will automatically receive an email updating them on their order status.

3. Searching Products on Doba's site

3.1 Copy the SKU from the Warhead invoice.

3.2 Open a new tab and login to the Doba supplier site.

3.3 Paste the Item No. into Doba search and select the item.

4. Placing an order on Doba's site

4.1 Click Add to Cart, go to cart, and Check Out using the Customer's Shipping Address from Warhead.

5. Changing the status from Processing to Shipped

5.1 Once you receive the tracking number from Doba, update the Warhead status to Shipped and Save.

6. Changing the status from Shipped to Completed

6.1 Once the customer has received the order, change the status to Completed.
